Ingredients:
 - 3 slices sugar free bacon
 - 1 leaf swiss chard
 - 1 leaf collard greens
 - 1 leaf Kale
 - 2 eggs 
 
Directions:
 1. Cook bacon on medium heat in a cast iron pan
 2. Chop all three greens, similar veggies can be used instead of these.
 3. after bacon is about half way to the doneness you like put greens on top of bacon
 4. Let cook for about 5 mintues, if bacon is to your doneness mix it in with greens.
 5. Push bacon and greens aside and break two eggs into pan and cover for 3 minutes.
 6. Plate it and enjoy, breakfast for one.
